Version: 1.0
Status: Active
Owner: Dr. Avdhut Kulkarni (Founder & Director)
Purpose
The Klinimate Clinical Notes Engine standardizes clinical documentation across every healthcare setting supported by the Klinimate Platform.
It enables healthcare professionals to create structured, searchable, AI-assisted clinical documentation while preserving the flexibility of free-text notes.
Every note becomes part of the patient's permanent clinical timeline.

Edit Policy
Clinical notes remain editable for 10 minutes after creation.
After 10 minutes:
Notes become permanently locked.
Existing text cannot be changed.
Corrections require a new clinical note.
This preserves clinical integrity while allowing correction of immediate typing errors.
