# Purpose

The Klinimate Clinical Notes Engine standardizes clinical documentation across every healthcare setting supported by the Klinimate Platform.

It enables healthcare professionals to create structured, searchable, AI-assisted documentation while preserving the flexibility of natural clinical writing.

Every note automatically becomes part of the patient's unified clinical timeline.

# Edit Policy

Clinical notes remain editable for **10 minutes** after creation.

After 10 minutes:

- Notes become permanently locked.
- Existing text cannot be modified.
- Corrections are recorded as new timeline entries.

This preserves both clinical integrity and legal auditability.

---
