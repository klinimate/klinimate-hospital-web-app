Klinimate Patient Timeline
Version: 1.0
Status: Active
Owner: Dr. Avdhut Kulkarni (Founder & Director)
Purpose
The Patient Timeline is the permanent chronological clinical record of every patient managed within the Klinimate Platform.
Every clinical event, assessment, investigation, treatment, consultation, and AI update is recorded in a single unified timeline regardless of the care setting.
The timeline provides a complete history of the patient's journey and serves as the primary reference for all healthcare professionals involved in patient care.

Guiding Principle
The Patient Timeline is the permanent clinical memory of the patient.
Every clinical interaction—whether performed by an organization clinician, a Klinimate Care Coordinator, a Klinimate Specialist, or Klinimate Intelligence—becomes part of one unified, chronological record that supports safe, coordinated, and continuous patient care.
