Klinimate Intelligence
Version: 1.0
Status: Active
Owner: Dr. Avdhut Kulkarni (Founder & Director)
Purpose
Klinimate Intelligence is the AI reasoning layer of the Klinimate Platform.
It transforms structured clinical information into actionable clinical intelligence by combining patient data, medical knowledge, evidence-based protocols, and AI-assisted reasoning.
Unlike the Knowledge Base, which stores medical knowledge, Klinimate Intelligence determines how the platform analyzes information, prioritizes clinical problems, supports decision-making, and recommends the next appropriate action.

Core Intelligence Modules
1. Clinical Summary Engine
Generates concise AI-generated summaries of the patient's condition.
2. Clinical Reasoning Engine
Analyzes symptoms, history, vital signs, investigations, medications, and clinical documents to generate patient-specific insights.
3. Risk Assessment Engine
Continuously evaluates patient risk and identifies clinical deterioration.
4. Clinical Intelligence Score
Calculates the overall clinical confidence and urgency based on available information.
5. Triage Engine
Assigns Klinimate Clinical Triage:
Green
Yellow
Red
Black
6. Differential Diagnosis Engine
Suggests possible differential diagnoses based on available clinical information.
7. Investigation Recommendation Engine
Suggests appropriate investigations using evidence-informed clinical reasoning.
8. Treatment Consideration Engine
Suggests high-level treatment considerations.
The engine supports clinicians but never replaces clinical judgment or independently prescribes treatment.
9. Consultation Engine
Determines when Klinimate specialist consultation is appropriate and recommends the most suitable specialty.
10. Workflow Engine
Guides the next step in the patient's clinical journey, including monitoring, follow-up, escalation, transfer, discharge, or specialist review.
11. Documentation Engine
Supports AI-assisted clinical documentation, including summaries, progress notes, discharge summaries, and referral letters.
12. Confidence Engine
Determines whether sufficient clinical information is available and recommends additional information when confidence is low.
13. Validation & Safety Engine
Validates AI-generated outputs against clinical safety rules before presenting recommendations.
