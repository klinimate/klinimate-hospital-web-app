Klinimate Notification & Escalation Engine
Version: 1.0
Status: Active
Owner: Dr. Avdhut Kulkarni (Founder & Director)
Purpose
The Klinimate Notification & Escalation Engine ensures that important clinical events are communicated promptly to the appropriate healthcare professionals.
The engine intelligently delivers notifications based on patient condition, clinical workflow, user role, and assigned responsibilities while minimizing unnecessary alerts.
Philosophy
Right Information
↓
Right Person
↓
Right Time
↓
Right Action
The goal is to improve patient safety while preventing alert fatigue.
Objectives
The engine should:
Detect clinically important events
Notify the appropriate users
Escalate when response is delayed
Reduce unnecessary interruptions
Improve coordination between healthcare teams
Maintain a complete audit trail
