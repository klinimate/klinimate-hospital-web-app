Klinimate Clinical Protocol Library
Version: 1.0
Status: Active
Owner: Dr. Avdhut Kulkarni (Founder & Director)
Purpose
The Klinimate Clinical Protocol Library provides standardized, evidence-informed clinical pathways to support healthcare professionals across all care settings.
The library is designed to assist clinicians by providing consistent assessment, investigation, management, escalation, and follow-up recommendations while allowing clinicians to exercise independent clinical judgement.
Clinical protocols are integrated with Klinimate Intelligence to support intelligent clinical decision-making.
Objectives
The Clinical Protocol Library aims to:
Standardize patient assessment
Reduce variation in clinical practice
Improve patient safety
Support junior clinicians
Assist experienced clinicians
Reduce missed clinical deterioration
Improve documentation quality
Support AI-assisted clinical workflows
Guiding Principles
Every protocol should:
Follow current evidence-based practice.
Be simple and practical.
Be usable on a mobile device.
Minimize unnecessary documentation.
Support—not replace—clinical judgement.
Be regularly reviewed and updated.

Integration with Klinimate Intelligence
The protocol library serves as one of the structured clinical knowledge sources used by Klinimate Intelligence.
When appropriate, the AI may:
Suggest relevant protocols
Highlight important clinical red flags
Recommend investigations
Recommend monitoring priorities
Suggest when specialist consultation should be considered
The AI must always present recommendations as supportive guidance rather than definitive clinical decisions.
Governance
Clinical protocols should be:
Evidence-based
Peer-reviewed
Version-controlled
Regularly updated
Transparent
Auditable

Future Vision
Over time, the Klinimate Clinical Protocol Library will evolve into a continuously updated digital clinical knowledge repository supporting healthcare professionals worldwide.
Combined with Klinimate Intelligence, it will deliver standardized, evidence-informed clinical guidance while respecting local practices, clinician expertise, and regulatory requirements.
Guiding Principle
Every Klinimate clinical recommendation should be grounded in standardized, evidence-informed clinical protocols while preserving the clinician's authority to make the final decision.
