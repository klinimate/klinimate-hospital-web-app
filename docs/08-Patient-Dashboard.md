Klinimate Patient Dashboard
Overview
The Patient Dashboard is the central clinical workspace within Klinimate.
Every healthcare professional—including hospital doctors, nurses, Klinimate Care Coordinators, Intensivists, and Specialists—works from the same patient record according to their assigned permissions.
The dashboard provides a complete chronological view of the patient's journey, regardless of whether the patient is being managed in OPD, Emergency, IPD, ICU, Rehabilitation, Home Care, or through a virtual consultation.
Dashboard Philosophy
The Patient Dashboard should answer three questions immediately:
Who is this patient?
What is happening now?
What should happen next?
The interface must prioritize patient information over branding and remain fast, intuitive, and mobile-friendly.
Patient Header
Display:
Patient Name
Age / Gender
Patient ID
Organization ID
Current Care Setting
Ward / Bed (if applicable)
Treating Consultant
Current Status
Registration Date & Time
Klinimate Clinical Intelligence Card
The AI Clinical Intelligence card appears at the top of the dashboard and includes:
AI Clinical Summary
Clinical Risk Assessment
Clinical Priorities
Suggested Differential Diagnoses
Suggested Investigations
Suggested Treatment Considerations
Clinical Intelligence Score
Recommendation to Request Klinimate Support (when appropriate)
This card updates automatically whenever new clinical information is added.
Vitals
Display the latest recorded vital signs.
Includes:
Blood Pressure
Pulse
Respiratory Rate
Temperature
Oxygen Saturation (SpO₂)
Blood Sugar
Glasgow Coma Scale (when applicable)
Urine Output (when applicable)
Weight
Pain Score
Trend indicators should highlight significant changes.
Clinical Notes
Clinical documentation follows a unified workflow.
Each note records:
Seen By
Role
Visit Type (In-person / Virtual)
Clinical Notes
Attached Clinical Documents
Date & Time
Notes are editable for 10 minutes, after which they become permanently locked. Any subsequent information is recorded as a new clinical update.
Clinical Documents
Users may attach:
Handwritten Notes
Laboratory Reports
ECGs
Chest X-rays
CT/MRI Reports
Referral Letters
Previous Prescriptions
Discharge Summaries
Clinical Photographs
Klinimate Intelligence analyzes supported documents and incorporates relevant findings into its assessment.
Investigations
Investigations are managed through a smart searchable library.
Each investigation displays:
Name
Status (Ordered / Pending / Completed)
Result Summary
Attached Report
Manual entry is always available.
Medications
Medications are managed using a smart searchable drug library.
Each medication includes:
Drug Name
Dose
Route
Frequency
Duration
Prescribing Clinician
Manual medication entry is supported when required.
General Advice
Quick-select advice options are available for common recommendations.
Examples:
Follow-up
Diet
Exercise
Hydration
Wound Care
Smoking Cessation
Users may also enter custom advice manually.
Virtual Specialist Consultation
When specialist support is required, clinicians can request consultation directly from the Patient Dashboard.
The consultation workflow includes:
Request Klinimate Consultation
Care Coordinator Assignment
Specialist Assignment
Virtual Consultation
Specialist Advice
Digital Prescription (where applicable)
Follow-up Plan
All consultation activities become part of the patient's timeline.
