Klinimate Patient Dashboard
Overview
The Patient Dashboard is the central clinical workspace within Klinimate and serves as the single source of truth for every patient.
Every authorized healthcare professional—including hospital doctors, nurses, Klinimate Care Coordinators, Intensivists, Specialists, and other assigned clinicians—works on the same patient record according to their role-based permissions.
The dashboard supports every stage of patient care using one universal workflow across:
OPD
Emergency
IPD
ICU
Rehabilitation
Home Care
Virtual Consultation
Regardless of where the patient is treated, all clinical information remains within a single longitudinal clinical record.
Dashboard Philosophy
The Patient Dashboard should immediately answer four questions:
Who is this patient?
What is the patient's current clinical status?
What is the current treatment plan?
What should happen next?
The dashboard should always prioritize patient information over branding and remain simple, mobile-first, and intuitive.

Klinimate Intelligence
The Klinimate Intelligence card is displayed at the top of every patient record.
It automatically updates whenever new information is added.
Displays:
AI Clinical Summary
Clinical Risk Assessment
Clinical Priorities
Suggested Differential Diagnoses
Suggested Investigations
Treatment Considerations
Clinical Intelligence Score
Clinical Alerts
Recommendation to Request Klinimate Support (when appropriate)
The AI supports clinicians but never replaces clinical judgement.

Users select:

Choose from:

Select from organization list
