Product Development Principles
Every sprint must:
Deliver a usable feature.
Reduce clinician workload.
Minimize documentation burden.
Improve patient safety.
Maintain a mobile-first experience.
Follow the principle: Click More • Type Less
Align with: One Patient • One Clinical Record • One Timeline
Ensure AI assists while clinicians remain in control.
