# Patient Dashboard

Version: 2.0

Status: Active

Owner: Dr. Avdhut Kulkarni (Founder & Director)

---

# Purpose

The Patient Dashboard is the primary clinical workspace within the Klinimate Platform.

It provides healthcare professionals with a complete view of the patient's current episode of care while enabling rapid clinical documentation, monitoring, collaboration, and decision-making.

Every authorized healthcare professional works from the same patient record according to role-based permissions.

---

# Dashboard Philosophy

The Patient Dashboard should immediately answer four questions:

- Who is this patient?
- What is happening now?
- What should happen next?
- What clinical action should I perform?

Patient Intelligence should always be displayed before detailed clinical information.

# Guiding Principle

The Patient Dashboard should provide every authorized healthcare professional with immediate access to the patient's current clinical condition, required actions, and complete clinical history while ensuring that Patient Intelligence remains the central decision-support tool throughout the patient's episode of care.
