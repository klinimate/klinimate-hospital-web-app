# 26 – Notification & Escalation Engine

**Version:** 2.0  
**Status:** Active  
**Owner:** Dr. Avdhut Kulkarni (Founder & Director)

---

# Purpose

The Klinimate Notification & Escalation Engine ensures that clinically significant events are communicated to the appropriate healthcare professionals at the right time.

The engine intelligently delivers notifications based on patient condition, Patient Intelligence, user role, assigned responsibilities, and organizational workflows while minimizing unnecessary interruptions.

Every notification and escalation is permanently recorded within the patient's clinical timeline.

---

# Objectives

The Notification & Escalation Engine is designed to:

- Improve patient safety
- Notify the right healthcare professional
- Support timely clinical intervention
- Coordinate Klinimate Support
- Reduce alert fatigue
- Improve communication across care teams
- Maintain complete auditability

---

# Notification Philosophy

# Notification Priority

## Information

Routine updates requiring no immediate action.

Examples:

- Patient Registered
- Clinical Note Added
- Investigation Ordered

---

## Attention

Clinical review recommended.

Examples:

- Investigation Result Available
- Follow-up Due
- Consultation Assigned

---

## Urgent

Timely clinical action required.

Examples:

- Clinical Risk Increased
- Critical Investigation Result
- Specialist Response Pending

---

## Critical

Immediate clinical action required.

Examples:

- Cardiac Arrest
- Respiratory Arrest
- Severe Clinical Deterioration
- Emergency Consultation

Critical notifications remain active until acknowledged.
