# Klinimate Knowledge Base

Version: 1.0

Status: Active

Owner: Dr. Avdhut Kulkarni (Founder & Director)

---

# Purpose

The Klinimate Knowledge Base is the centralized medical knowledge repository that powers Klinimate Intelligence.

It contains structured, evidence-informed clinical knowledge used to support AI-assisted clinical decision-making across every healthcare setting supported by the Klinimate Platform.

Unlike the Klinimate Intelligence Engine, which determines how AI reasons and responds, the Knowledge Base stores what the AI knows.

---

# Philosophy

Medical Knowledge

↓

Structured Information

↓

Clinical Intelligence

↓

Better Patient Care

The Knowledge Base provides the factual medical foundation upon which Klinimate Intelligence generates clinical insights.

---

# Objectives

The Knowledge Base aims to:

- Standardize medical knowledge across the platform.
- Support evidence-informed clinical decision support.
- Reduce variability in AI recommendations.
- Enable rapid expansion into new specialties.
- Separate medical knowledge from AI reasoning.
- Support continuous improvement through expert review.

---

# Knowledge Domains

The Knowledge Base is organized into the following domains:

## 1. Clinical Protocols

Evidence-informed management pathways for common clinical conditions.

Examples:

- Sepsis
- Acute Coronary Syndrome
- Stroke
- Acute Respiratory Failure
- COPD Exacerbation
- Heart Failure
- Dengue

---

## 2. Diseases

Structured disease profiles including:

- Definition
- Clinical Presentation
- Risk Factors
- Differential Diagnoses
- Diagnostic Criteria
- Complications
- Monitoring
- Follow-up

---

## 3. Investigations

Structured information for laboratory and diagnostic investigations.

Includes:

- Clinical indications
- Interpretation principles
- Critical values
- Common abnormalities
- Clinical significance

Examples:

- CBC
- ABG
- ECG
- Chest X-ray
- CT Brain
- MRI

---

## 4. Medications

Structured medication reference including:

- Generic Name
- Drug Class
- Indications
- Contraindications
- Routes
- Dose Ranges
- Adverse Effects
- Drug Interactions
- Monitoring Requirements

Medication information supports clinicians but does not replace prescribing judgement.

---

## 5. Medical Imaging

Knowledge related to:

- ECG
- Chest X-ray
- CT
- MRI
- Ultrasound
- Clinical Photographs

Future expansion includes echocardiography and pathology.

---

## 6. Clinical Guidelines

Evidence sources including:

- WHO
- National Guidelines
- Specialty Society Guidelines
- Hospital Clinical Policies

---

## 7. Medical Calculators

Reference material for validated clinical scoring systems.

Examples:

- NEWS2
- qSOFA
- SOFA
- CURB-65
- Glasgow Coma Scale
- Wells Score

---

## 8. Medical Terminology

Standardized terminology including:

- SNOMED CT
- ICD
- LOINC
- Common Medical Abbreviations

---

# Governance

Every knowledge resource includes:

- Version
- Author
- Reviewer
- Approval Status
- Published Date
- Last Updated
- Review Schedule

All content should undergo clinical review before release.

---

# Relationship with Klinimate Intelligence

The Knowledge Base provides structured medical knowledge.

Klinimate Intelligence retrieves relevant knowledge, applies clinical reasoning, evaluates patient-specific information, and generates AI-assisted recommendations.

Knowledge Base = What the AI Knows

Klinimate Intelligence = How the AI Thinks
