Klinimate Brand Guidelines
Version: 1.0
Status: Active
Owner: Dr. Avdhut Kulkarni (Founder & Director)
Purpose
This document defines the official visual identity, brand language, naming conventions, and user experience standards for the Klinimate ecosystem.
Every Klinimate product—including web applications, mobile applications, AI services, command centre, virtual specialist network, websites, APIs, and marketing material—must follow these standards.
This document serves as the single source of truth for all Klinimate branding.
Brand Identity
Klinimate is an AI-Powered Clinical Intelligence Platform that simplifies healthcare through intelligent clinical workflows, AI-assisted decision support, standardized documentation, and seamless collaboration between healthcare professionals.
The brand should communicate:
Trust
Clinical Excellence
Intelligence
Simplicity
Reliability
Innovation
Global Healthcare
Every interaction should feel calm, premium, modern, and effortless.
Vision
To become the world's most trusted AI-Powered Clinical Intelligence Platform, empowering every healthcare organization with intelligent, standardized, specialist-supported patient care.

Brand Naming Convention
To maintain a consistent global identity, the following terminology must always be used.
Klinimate
"Klinimate" refers to the company, platform, products, and ecosystem.
Examples
Klinimate Platform
Klinimate Website
Klinimate Mobile App
Klinimate Admin
Klinimate Command Centre
Klinimate Virtual Specialist Network
Klinimate Intelligence
"Klinimate Intelligence" refers exclusively to the Artificial Intelligence engine powering the platform.
Examples
Klinimate Intelligence generated the Clinical Summary.
Klinimate Intelligence analysed the uploaded laboratory report.
Klinimate Intelligence recommends requesting specialist support.
Klinimate Intelligence updated the Clinical Risk Assessment.
Avoid referring to AI outputs using only "Klinimate."

Colour Philosophy
Brand Colours communicate identity.
Clinical Colours communicate clinical status.
The two colour systems must never be mixed.

Branding Rule
There is only one official Klinimate brand identity.
No developer, designer, agency, or AI assistant may introduce alternative logos, colour palettes, typography, icons, or branding styles without approval from the Founder.
Every Klinimate product must consistently follow the principles defined in this document.
Guiding Principle
Klinimate is the platform.
Klinimate Intelligence is the AI.
Every design decision should simplify healthcare, strengthen clinician trust, and make intelligent patient care accessible through one unified clinical experience.
