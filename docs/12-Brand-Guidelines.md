Version: 1.0
Status: Active
Owner: Dr. Avdhut Kulkarni (Founder & Director)

---

# Purpose

This document defines the official visual identity of Klinimate.

All current and future products—including the Klinimate Hospital Platform, Klinimate Command Centre, Klinimate Patient App, Website, Mobile Applications, Admin Portal, and Marketing Material—must follow these branding standards.

No developer, designer, or AI assistant should introduce new colors, logos, or visual styles that conflict with this document.

---

# Brand Identity

Klinimate is an AI-powered Clinical Command Centre designed for hospitals.

The visual identity should communicate:

• Trust
• Clinical Excellence
• Simplicity
• Reliability
• Intelligence
• Modern Healthcare Technology

The interface should feel calm, clean, premium, and enterprise-grade.

Avoid decorative or playful design.

# Clinical Colors

Clinical colors represent patient condition.

These colors must NEVER be used for branding.

Green

Meaning:

Stable Patient

Yellow

Meaning:

Urgent Patient

Red

Meaning:

Emergency Patient

Black

Meaning:

Black Triage / Deceased

Clinical colors must remain consistent throughout the application.

---

# Color Philosophy

Brand Colors

Navy Blue = Klinimate Identity

Teal = User Actions

Clinical Colors

Green = Stable

Yellow = Urgent

Red = Emergency

Black = Black Category

Brand colors and clinical colors must never be mixed.

# Branding Rule

There is only ONE official Klinimate visual identity.
