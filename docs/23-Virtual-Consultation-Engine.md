# 21 – Virtual Consultation Engine

**Version:** 2.0  
**Status:** Active  
**Owner:** Dr. Avdhut Kulkarni (Founder & Director)

---

# Purpose

The Klinimate Virtual Consultation Engine enables healthcare organizations to connect with Klinimate specialists through a standardized, secure, and AI-assisted consultation workflow.

Every consultation is fully integrated into the patient's clinical record, ensuring continuity of care without creating separate consultation systems.

---

# Objectives

The Virtual Consultation Engine is designed to:

- Provide timely access to specialist expertise
- Support local healthcare professionals
- Reduce unnecessary patient transfers
- Standardize virtual consultation workflows
- Improve clinical decision-making
- Reduce documentation burden
- Coordinate specialist care efficiently
- Maintain one continuous patient timeline

---

# Requesting Klinimate Support

Support may be requested by:

- Hospital Doctor
- Hospital Consultant
- Hospital Nurse *(if permitted by organization policy)*
- Klinimate Care Coordinator
- Klinimate Intensivist

Each request includes:

- Specialty Required
- Priority
- Clinical Question
- Reason for Consultation
- Supporting Clinical Information

The request is submitted using the **📞 Request Klinimate Support** button within the Patient Dashboard.

---

# Klinimate Care Coordinator

The Care Coordinator manages the complete consultation workflow.

Responsibilities include:

- Review consultation requests
- Verify clinical information
- Request additional information when required
- Assign the appropriate specialist
- Coordinate communication
- Schedule consultations
- Track response times
- Ensure timely completion
- Escalate urgent consultations

# Specialist Documentation

Following consultation, the specialist records:

- Clinical Assessment
- Impression / Diagnosis
- Recommendations
- Suggested Investigations
- Medication Recommendations
- General Advice
- Follow-up Plan

Where permitted by local regulations, a digital prescription may also be generated.

All documentation is automatically added to the patient's timeline.

---

# Consultation Status

Every consultation progresses through the following stages:

```
Requested
        ↓
Under Review
        ↓
Assigned
        ↓
Scheduled
        ↓
In Progress
        ↓
Completed
        ↓
Follow-up Required (Optional)
```

Status updates are visible to both the healthcare organization and Klinimate teams.

- Request Date & Time
- Requesting User
- Assigned Care Coordinator
- Assigned Specialist
- Consultation Mode
- Consultation Date & Time
- Specialist Notes
- Recommendations
- Prescription (if applicable)
- Follow-up Plan

# Response Time Monitoring

- Time to Consultation

# Integration with Klinimate Intelligence

Before the consultation begins, Klinimate Intelligence prepares:

- AI Clinical Summary
- Clinical Risk Assessment
- Clinical Priorities
- Suggested Differential Diagnoses
- Suggested Investigations
- Treatment Considerations
- Clinical Alerts
- Clinical Intelligence Score

This enables specialists to rapidly understand the patient's condition before the consultation.

AI recommendations remain advisory.

---

# Security & Clinical Governance

All consultations are:

- Authenticated
- Role-Based
- Encrypted
- Time-Stamped
- Audit Logged

Every action is permanently recorded.

Clinical documentation follows the standard Klinimate editing policy:

- Editable for 10 minutes
- Locked thereafter
- Future changes recorded as new timeline entries
