# Klinimate Virtual Specialist Network (VSN)

Version: 2.0

Status: Active

Owner: Dr. Avdhut Kulkarni (Founder & Director)

---

# Purpose

The Klinimate Virtual Specialist Network (VSN) enables healthcare organizations to connect with experienced Klinimate specialists through one unified clinical platform.

The network provides timely specialist support coordinated by the Klinimate Care Coordinator, allowing healthcare organizations to access expert clinical advice without requiring specialists to be physically present.

Every consultation becomes part of the patient's permanent clinical record and Patient Timeline.

---

# Network Philosophy

The Virtual Specialist Network exists to make specialist expertise accessible to every healthcare organization, regardless of location.

The network combines:

- Patient Intelligence
- Klinimate Care Coordinators
- Virtual Specialists
- AI-assisted Clinical Intelligence

to deliver timely, coordinated, and specialist-supported patient care.

---

# Objectives

The Virtual Specialist Network aims to:

- Provide rapid access to specialist expertise
- Reduce unnecessary patient transfers
- Support local healthcare teams
- Standardize specialist consultation workflows
- Improve patient outcomes
- Reduce documentation burden
- Enable one specialist to support multiple organizations
- Deliver coordinated AI-assisted care

# User Roles

## Healthcare Organization

Responsibilities

- Request Klinimate Support
- Register patients
- Update patient information
- Record clinical documentation
- Upload clinical documents
- Implement specialist recommendations
- Continue patient management

---

## Klinimate Care Coordinator

Responsibilities

- Receive support requests
- Review Patient Intelligence
- Ensure clinical information is complete
- Coordinate communication
- Assign the most appropriate specialist
- Monitor response times
- Track consultation progress
- Ensure follow-up completion

The Care Coordinator serves as the central communication hub between healthcare organizations and the Klinimate specialist network.

---

## Klinimate Virtual Specialist

Responsibilities

- Review Patient Intelligence
- Review Medical Notes
- Review Nursing Notes
- Review Vitals
- Review Intake & Output
- Review Investigations
- Review Medications
- Review Clinical Documents
- Conduct virtual consultation
- Provide specialist recommendations
- Recommend medication changes
- Recommend investigations
- Recommend follow-up
- Complete consultation

↓

↓

↓

Each specialist views only assigned consultations.

Displays

- Assigned Patients
- Pending Consultations
- Active Consultations
- Completed Consultations
- Follow-up Reviews
- Notifications

---

# Patient Review

The specialist reviews one unified patient record.

Displays

- Patient Intelligence
- Current Clinical Status
- Medical Notes
- Nursing Notes
- Vitals
- Intake & Output
- Investigations
- Medications
- Clinical Documents
- Previous Specialist Reviews
- Patient Timeline

# Integration with Patient Intelligence

Every consultation contributes to Patient Intelligence.

Specialist recommendations automatically update:

- Clinical Summary
- Clinical Priorities
- Follow-up Plan
- Clinical Timeline

Before discharge, these recommendations become part of the finalized Patient Intelligence Summary.

---

# Communication Principles

The consultation workflow should be:

- Mobile-first
- Simple
- Fast
- Secure
- AI-assisted
- Minimal documentation
- Coordinated through the Care Coordinator

Whenever possible, consultation begins with a standard phone call and may be upgraded to a video consultation when clinically necessary.
