Klinimate Virtual Specialist Network
Version: 1.0
Status: Active
Overview
The Klinimate Virtual Specialist Network (VSN) enables healthcare organizations to connect with experienced Klinimate specialists through one unified clinical platform.
The network provides timely specialist consultations, coordinated through the Klinimate Care Coordinator, allowing hospitals and healthcare organizations to access expert clinical advice without requiring specialists to be physically present.
All consultations occur within the patient's unified clinical timeline.
Objectives
The Virtual Specialist Network aims to:
Provide timely access to specialist expertise
Reduce unnecessary patient transfers
Support local clinical teams
Standardize specialist consultation workflows
Improve patient outcomes
Reduce documentation burden
Enable one specialist to support multiple healthcare organizations
Deliver coordinated, AI-assisted clinical care

Guiding Principle
The Virtual Specialist Network is designed to make specialist expertise accessible to every healthcare organization, regardless of location.
By combining Klinimate Intelligence, Care Coordinators, and a global network of specialists, the platform enables hospitals, clinics, rehabilitation centres, nursing homes, and home care providers to deliver coordinated, specialist-supported patient care through a single, unified clinical workflow.
